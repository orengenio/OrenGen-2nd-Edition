2. Support Plans & SLAs

2.1 Starter Plan Support

  • Email Support: Response within 48 hours (business days)
  • Live Chat: Business hours only
  • Knowledge Base: Full access
  • Community Forum: Access to peer support
  • Phone Support: Not included

2.2 Professional Plan Support

  • Priority Email: Response within 4 hours (business hours)
  • 24/7 Live Chat: Available round-the-clock
  • Phone Support: Business hours
  • Screen Sharing: For troubleshooting sessions
  • Quarterly Business Reviews: Optional check-ins

2.3 Enterprise Plan Support

  • 24/7 Support: All channels available around the clock
  • Critical Issue Response: Within 1 hour
  • Dedicated Account Manager: Single point of contact
  • Dedicated Slack Channel: Direct access to support team
  • Onboarding Specialist: Guided implementation
  • Custom Training: Tailored to your team's needs
  • Monthly Business Reviews: Strategic planning sessions

3. Response Time SLAs

3.1 Severity Levels

Severity 1 - Critical

  • Definition: Complete service outage affecting all users
  • Professional Plan: 4-hour response, 24-hour resolution target
  • Enterprise Plan: 1-hour response, 8-hour resolution target

Severity 2 - Major

  • Definition: Significant feature unavailable or performance degradation
  • Professional Plan: 8-hour response, 3-day resolution target
  • Enterprise Plan: 2-hour response, 1-day resolution target

Severity 3 - Minor

  • Definition: Minor feature issue with workaround available
  • Professional Plan: 24-hour response, 7-day resolution target
  • Enterprise Plan: 4-hour response, 3-day resolution target

Severity 4 - Informational

  • Definition: General questions, feature requests, documentation
  • All Plans: 48-hour response (business days)
