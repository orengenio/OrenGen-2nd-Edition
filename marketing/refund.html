Refund Policy

Last Updated: January 1, 2026

This Refund Policy outlines OrenGen.io's position on refunds, cancellations, and service disputes for all AI solutions and services.

Important Notice: All sales are final. We do not offer refunds for any of our services or products.

1. No Refund Policy

1.1 General Policy

All sales are final. OrenGen.io does not provide refunds for any services, subscriptions, or products under any circumstances. This includes but is not limited to:

  • Monthly subscription plans
  • Annual subscription plans
  • Custom development services
  • Enterprise agreements
  • One-time purchases
  • Add-on features or upgrades

1.2 Reasons for No Refund Policy

This policy exists because:

  • Immediate Service Access: Our AI services are delivered digitally and accessible immediately upon purchase
  • Resource Allocation: We allocate computing resources, AI model capacity, and infrastructure based on your subscription
  • Custom Development: Many services involve custom AI training and configuration specific to your needs
  • Data Processing: Your data is processed through our AI systems, which incurs real costs

2. Free Trial & Testing Options

To help you make an informed decision before purchasing, we offer:

2.1 Free Trial Period

  • 14-Day Free Trial: Available for Starter and Professional plans
  • No Credit Card Required: Test our services without payment information
  • Full Feature Access: Experience all plan features during the trial
  • Usage Limits: Trial accounts have reasonable usage caps to prevent abuse

2.2 Demo Sessions

  • Live Demonstrations: Schedule a personalized demo with our team
  • Proof of Concept: Enterprise clients can request a POC project
  • Sandbox Environment: Test our APIs in a controlled environment

2.3 Money-Back Guarantee Alternative

While we don't offer refunds, we provide a satisfaction guarantee:

  • If you're not satisfied within the first 14 days, we'll work with you to resolve issues
  • We'll provide additional training, support, and configuration assistance
  • If we cannot meet your needs, we'll help you transition to an alternative solution

3. Cancellation Policy

3.1 How to Cancel

You may cancel your subscription at any time:

  • Self-Service: Cancel directly from your account dashboard
  • Email Request: Send cancellation request to billing@orengen.io
  • Customer Support: Contact our support team for assistance

3.2 Cancellation Effective Date

  • Monthly Plans: Cancellation takes effect at the end of the current billing cycle
  • Annual Plans: Cancellation takes effect at the end of the annual term
  • Enterprise Plans: Subject to contract terms (typically 30-90 days notice)

3.3 Access After Cancellation

  • You retain full access to services until the end of your paid period
  • After cancellation, you can export your data for 30 days
  • Data is permanently deleted 90 days after cancellation

3.4 No Partial Refunds

Important: Cancellation does not entitle you to a refund for the unused portion of your subscription. You have already paid for access through the end of the billing period and will retain that access.

4. Billing Disputes

4.1 Reporting Billing Errors

If you believe you've been billed incorrectly:

  • Contact billing@orengen.io within 30 days of the charge
  • Provide your account details and a description of the error
  • Include any supporting documentation (screenshots, receipts, etc.)

4.2 Legitimate Billing Errors

We will issue refunds for genuine billing errors, including:

  • Duplicate charges
  • Charges after verified cancellation
  • Incorrect billing amounts due to system error
  • Charges to closed accounts
  • Unauthorized transactions

4.3 Chargeback Policy

Warning: Initiating a chargeback instead of contacting us first may result in:

  • Immediate account suspension
  • Termination of services
  • Loss of all data without recovery option
  • Permanent ban from using OrenGen.io services
  • Legal action to recover chargeback fees and damages

Always contact us first to resolve billing disputes amicably.

5. Service Outages & Technical Issues

5.1 Service Credits

Instead of refunds, we may issue service credits for significant outages:

  • Professional Plan: Credits for outages exceeding SLA (99.9% uptime)
  • Enterprise Plan: Credits per contract terms (99.95% uptime guarantee)
  • Credit Calculation: Based on percentage of downtime and monthly fee

5.2 SLA Violations

Service Level Agreement violations may result in:

  • Account credits applied to next billing cycle
  • Extended service period at no additional cost
  • Upgraded plan features temporarily or permanently

5.3 Technical Support

For technical issues:

  • Contact support@orengen.io immediately
  • We'll work to resolve issues within SLA response times
  • Critical issues receive priority support

6. Downgrade & Plan Changes

6.1 Downgrading Your Plan

  • You may downgrade to a lower-tier plan at any time
  • Downgrade takes effect at the next billing cycle
  • No refund for the difference in plan pricing
  • You retain current plan features until downgrade is effective

6.2 Upgrading Your Plan

  • Upgrades take effect immediately
  • You are charged a prorated amount for the current billing cycle
  • Next billing cycle reflects the new plan price

7. Enterprise & Custom Contracts

7.1 Enterprise Agreement Terms

Enterprise contracts have specific terms:

  • Minimum contract periods (typically 12-36 months)
  • Early termination fees may apply
  • Custom payment schedules and invoicing
  • Specific SLA and service credit terms

7.2 Custom Development Projects

  • Milestone-based payments are non-refundable once work begins
  • Completed work is billed regardless of project cancellation
  • IP rights are transferred only after full payment

8. Force Majeure & Service Discontinuation

8.1 Service Discontinuation

If OrenGen.io discontinues a service:

  • We'll provide 90 days advance notice
  • You'll receive assistance migrating to alternative solutions
  • Prorated refunds may be issued for prepaid annual plans
  • Enterprise contracts have specific termination clauses

8.2 Force Majeure Events

We're not liable for service interruptions due to:

  • Natural disasters
  • War, terrorism, or civil unrest
  • Government actions or regulations
  • Cyber attacks beyond our control
  • Third-party service failures (AWS, cloud providers)

9. Exceptions

The only circumstances under which refunds may be considered:

9.1 Legal Requirements

  • Refunds required by applicable law
  • Consumer protection regulations in your jurisdiction
  • Court orders or regulatory mandates

9.2 Executive Exceptions

  • Rare cases approved by executive management
  • Extraordinary circumstances evaluated case-by-case
  • Goodwill gestures at our sole discretion
