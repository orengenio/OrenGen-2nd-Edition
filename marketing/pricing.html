Frequently Asked Questions

Can I change plans later?

Absolutely! You can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time. When upgrading, you'll get immediate access to new features. When downgrading, changes take effect at the start of your next billing cycle.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express, Discover), PayPal, bank transfers (ACH/wire), and cryptocurrency for annual plans. Enterprise clients can also request invoice billing.

Is there a free trial available?

Yes! We offer a 14-day free trial on both Starter and Professional plans. No credit card required. You'll get full access to all features to ensure our solution is the right fit for your business.

What happens if I exceed my plan limits?

We'll notify you when you reach 80% and 100% of your limits. You can either upgrade to a higher tier or purchase additional interaction packs. We never shut off your service unexpectedly.

Do you offer refunds?

Yes, we offer a 30-day money-back guarantee on all annual plans. If you're not completely satisfied, we'll provide a full refund—no questions asked. Monthly plans are non-refundable but can be cancelled anytime.

Are there any setup fees or hidden costs?

No hidden fees. The price you see is the price you pay. Enterprise plans may include optional professional services for custom development, but these are always discussed and agreed upon upfront.

How does support work for each plan?

Starter plans get email support with 48-hour response times. Professional plans get priority email support with 4-hour response times during business hours. Enterprise plans get 24/7 dedicated support with 1-hour response times and a dedicated account manager.

Can I get a custom plan?

Yes! If our standard plans don't fit your needs, we can create a custom package. This is common for enterprises with specific requirements, regulatory needs, or unique use cases. Contact our sales team to discuss your needs.
