Getting Started

Step-by-step onboarding and setup for OrenGen AI solutions.

Quick Setup Guide

  1. Create your OrenGen account - Sign up at orengen.io and verify your email address
  2. Verify your email and set up security - Enable two-factor authentication for enhanced security
  3. Choose your AI agent or service - Select from Buy-Lingual™ AI Agents, AI Call Center, Lead Generation, or CRM Automation
  4. Connect integrations - Link your CRM, phone system, and other business tools
  5. Launch your first workflow - Configure your AI agent and go live in minutes
