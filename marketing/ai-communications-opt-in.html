AI Communications Opt-In

Last Updated: January 1, 2026

Manage your preferences for AI-powered communications, notifications, and interactions with OrenGen services.

Your Choice: You have full control over AI-powered communications from OrenGen. This page explains your options and how to manage your preferences.

1. Overview

OrenGen.io uses AI-powered systems to communicate with you through various channels. This includes:

  • AI voice agents for customer support and sales
  • AI chatbots on our website and platforms
  • Automated email responses and notifications
  • AI-generated content recommendations
  • Predictive assistance and proactive support

2. Types of AI Communications

2.1 Service Communications

These are essential AI communications required for service delivery:

  • Account notifications: Password resets, security alerts, billing updates
  • Service updates: System maintenance, feature releases, critical announcements
  • Support interactions: AI chatbot responses to your inquiries

Note: You cannot opt-out of essential service communications while maintaining an active account.

2.2 Marketing Communications

These are promotional AI communications you can control:

  • AI-generated product recommendations
  • Personalized feature suggestions
  • Marketing emails with AI-curated content
  • AI voice calls for sales and promotions

2.3 AI Voice Interactions

  • Inbound support calls handled by AI agents
  • Outbound sales calls from AI representatives
  • Appointment reminders via AI voice
  • Survey and feedback collection calls

3. Your Rights & Choices

3.1 Opt-In Requirements

We require explicit opt-in consent for:

  • Marketing AI Voice Calls: You must provide consent before receiving promotional calls from our AI agents
  • AI SMS/Text Messages: Express consent required for text-based AI communications
  • Third-Party AI Sharing: Your data being used by partner AI systems

3.2 Opt-Out Options

You can opt-out of:

  • All marketing communications (email, voice, SMS)
  • AI-powered personalization features
  • Data sharing for AI model training
  • Specific communication channels

4. How to Manage Your Preferences

4.1 Account Dashboard

  1. Log in to your OrenGen account
  2. Navigate to Settings → Communication Preferences
  3. Toggle AI communication options on/off
  4. Save your preferences

4.2 Email Preferences

  • Click "Unsubscribe" at the bottom of any marketing email
  • Visit our Preference Center to customize settings
  • Email privacy@orengen.io with specific requests

4.3 Voice Call Opt-Out

To stop receiving AI voice calls:

  • Say "opt-out" or "remove me from your call list" during any AI call
  • Call +1 (817) 555-0100 and speak with our team
  • Update preferences in your account dashboard
  • Email donotcall@orengen.io

4.4 SMS/Text Opt-Out

  • Reply "STOP" to any AI-generated text message
  • Update SMS preferences in your account settings
  • Text messages will cease within 24 hours

5. Compliance & Regulations

5.1 TCPA Compliance

We comply with the Telephone Consumer Protection Act (TCPA):

  • We obtain express written consent before making marketing calls
  • We honor opt-out requests immediately
  • We maintain internal do-not-call lists
  • We respect time-of-day restrictions

5.2 CAN-SPAM Compliance

Our AI-generated emails comply with CAN-SPAM requirements:

  • Clear identification as marketing messages
  • Accurate "From" and subject lines
  • Valid physical address included
  • Easy opt-out mechanism provided
  • Opt-out requests honored within 10 business days

5.3 GDPR & International Compliance

  • Explicit consent required for EU residents
  • Easy withdrawal of consent at any time
  • Clear disclosure of AI usage
  • Right to access and delete communication data

6. AI Transparency

6.1 AI Disclosure

We clearly disclose when you're interacting with AI:

  • AI voice agents identify themselves at the start of calls
  • Chatbots display "AI Assistant" labels
  • Email footers indicate AI-generated content
  • You can always request human interaction

6.2 Human Escalation

You can request to speak with a human at any time during AI interactions by:

  • Saying "speak to a human" or "transfer to agent"
  • Clicking "Connect to Human" in chat interfaces
  • Emailing support@orengen.io

7. Data Usage & Privacy

7.1 Communication Data Collection

When you interact with our AI systems, we collect:

  • Conversation transcripts and recordings
  • Interaction patterns and preferences
  • Response times and satisfaction metrics
  • Voice biometrics (with consent)

7.2 Data Retention

  • Service communications: Retained for account lifetime + 1 year
  • Marketing communications: Retained until opt-out + 30 days
  • Voice recordings: Deleted after 90 days (unless flagged for quality assurance)
  • Anonymized data: May be retained indefinitely for AI improvement

7.3 Third-Party Sharing

We do NOT share your communication data with third parties for their marketing purposes without explicit consent.
