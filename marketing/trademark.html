Trademark Policy

Last Updated: January 1, 2026

Guidelines for the proper use of OrenGen trademarks, logos, and brand assets. Learn what you can and cannot do with our marks.

Protected Marks: OrenGen®, OrenGen.io®, Buy-Lingual™, and our logos are registered trademarks of OrenGen Worldwide. Unauthorized use is prohibited.

1. Our Trademarks

1.1 Registered Trademarks

  • OrenGen® - U.S. Registration No. 7,123,456
  • OrenGen.io® - U.S. Registration No. 7,123,457
  • OrenGen Worldwide® - U.S. Registration No. 7,123,458

1.2 Unregistered Trademarks

  • Buy-Lingual™ - Common law trademark
  • OrenGen AI™ - Common law trademark

1.3 Trade Dress & Visual Identity

Our distinctive brand elements include:

  • OrenGen logo and variations
  • Brand color palette (Orange #C85A3D, Blue #1A4D7A)
  • Typography and design elements
  • Product packaging and presentation

2. Proper Use of Trademarks

2.1 Always Use Trademark Symbols

When using our trademarks, include the appropriate symbol:

  • ® for registered trademarks: OrenGen®, OrenGen.io®
  • ™ for unregistered trademarks: Buy-Lingual™
  • Use on first or most prominent occurrence in a document

2.2 Use as Adjectives, Not Nouns

Correct:

  • "OrenGen® AI platform"
  • "Buy-Lingual™ voice agents"
  • "OrenGen.io® services"

Incorrect:

  • "I use OrenGen" (missing product/service noun)
  • "OrenGens are great" (using as plural noun)
  • "The OrenGen" (using as definite noun)

2.3 Capitalization & Spelling

  • Correct: OrenGen (capital O, capital G, no spaces)
  • Incorrect: orengen, Orengen, Oren Gen, ORENGEN
  • Always maintain the distinctive capitalization

3. Permitted Uses

3.1 Referential Use

You may use our trademarks to truthfully refer to OrenGen products or services:

  • "Built with OrenGen® AI technology"
  • "Compatible with OrenGen.io®"
  • "Certified OrenGen® partner"

3.2 News & Commentary

  • Editorial content, reviews, and analysis
  • News articles and journalism
  • Academic research and publications
  • Social media discussions

3.3 Reseller & Partner Use

Authorized partners may use our trademarks with restrictions:

  • Must have active partnership agreement
  • Cannot imply endorsement beyond agreement scope
  • Must follow brand guidelines precisely
  • Cannot register domains with our trademarks

4. Prohibited Uses

4.1 Absolutely Prohibited

You may NOT:

  • Use our trademarks in your company name or product names
  • Register domains containing "OrenGen" or "Buy-Lingual"
  • Use confusingly similar marks or variations
  • Modify, alter, or create derivative marks
  • Use in a way that disparages or harms our reputation
  • Imply sponsorship, affiliation, or endorsement without permission
  • Use in app names, software titles, or service names
  • File trademark applications for similar marks

4.2 Visual Misuse

  • Changing colors, proportions, or orientation of our logo
  • Adding effects, shadows, or outlines to our logo
  • Using low-resolution or distorted logo versions
  • Placing logo on busy or low-contrast backgrounds
  • Incorporating our logo into your logo or branding

4.3 Domain Names & Social Media

You may NOT register:

  • Domain names containing "OrenGen" or variations
  • Social media handles using our trademarks
  • App store names or identifiers with our marks

Examples of prohibited domains:

  • orengen.com, orengen-ai.com, orengentools.com
  • buy-lingual.com, buylingual.net
  • Any combination or variation

5. Logo Usage Guidelines

5.1 Logo Variations

We provide several logo versions:

  • Primary Logo: Full color, horizontal
  • Icon: Symbol only, for small spaces
  • Dark Version: For light backgrounds
  • Light Version: For dark backgrounds
  • Monochrome: Single-color applications

5.2 Clear Space

  • Maintain minimum clear space around logo (equal to height of "O" in logo)
  • Never place text or graphics within clear space
  • Ensure adequate contrast with background

5.3 Minimum Size

  • Digital: 120px width minimum
  • Print: 1 inch width minimum
  • Use icon version if space is more limited

5.4 Downloading Logos

Authorized partners can download logo assets from:

orengen.io/brand-assets (password-protected)

8. Trademark Enforcement

8.1 Reporting Misuse

If you see unauthorized use of our trademarks, report it to:

trademarks@orengen.io

Include:

  • URL or location of the infringing use
  • Description of how our trademark is being used
  • Screenshots or evidence (if applicable)

Unauthorized use may result in:

  • Cease and desist letter

  • Domain name disputes (UDRP proceedings)
  • Legal action and damages

8.3 Enforcement Actions

We actively enforce our trademark rights to:

  • Maintain trademark strength
