Cookie Policy

Last Updated: January 1, 2026

This Cookie Policy explains how OrenGen.io uses cookies and similar tracking technologies on our website and services.

Quick Summary: We use cookies to improve your experience, remember your preferences, and analyze site usage. You can control cookie settings in your browser.

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are placed on your device (computer, smartphone, tablet) when you visit our website. They help us:

  • Remember your preferences and settings
  • Understand how you use our website
  • Improve your user experience
  • Provide personalized content and features
  • Keep our services secure

2. Types of Cookies We Use

2.1 Essential Cookies

These cookies are necessary for the website to function properly. They cannot be disabled.

  • Session Cookies: Keep you logged in during your visit
  • Security Cookies: Protect against fraud and unauthorized access
  • Load Balancing: Distribute traffic across our servers

2.2 Performance Cookies

These cookies help us understand how visitors interact with our website.

  • Analytics: Google Analytics, Mixpanel, Amplitude
  • Page Load Times: Monitor site performance
  • Error Tracking: Identify and fix technical issues

2.3 Functional Cookies

These cookies enable enhanced functionality and personalization.

  • Language Preferences: Remember your language selection
  • Theme Settings: Dark mode, layout preferences
  • Chat Widget: Remember chat conversations
  • Video Playback: Remember volume and quality settings

2.4 Marketing Cookies

These cookies track your activity to deliver relevant advertisements.

  • Advertising Networks: Google Ads, Facebook Pixel, LinkedIn Insight Tag
  • Retargeting: Show relevant ads based on pages visited
  • Conversion Tracking: Measure ad campaign effectiveness

3. Third-Party Cookies

We use services from trusted third parties that may set cookies on your device:

3.1 Analytics Providers

  • Google Analytics: Website traffic and user behavior analysis
  • Hotjar: Heatmaps and session recordings
  • Mixpanel: Product usage analytics

3.2 Marketing & Advertising

  • Google Ads: Display advertising and remarketing
  • Facebook Pixel: Social media advertising
  • LinkedIn Insight Tag: B2B advertising and analytics
  • Twitter Pixel: Social media marketing

3.3 Customer Support

  • Intercom: Live chat and customer support
  • Zendesk: Help desk and ticketing system

3.4 Content Delivery

  • Cloudflare: CDN and security services
  • YouTube: Video content embedding
  • Vimeo: Video hosting and playback

4. How Long Do Cookies Last?

4.1 Session Cookies

Temporary cookies deleted when you close your browser

  • Shopping cart contents
  • Login session tokens
  • Form data in progress

4.2 Persistent Cookies

Remain on your device for a set period or until manually deleted

  • 30 days: Marketing and advertising cookies
  • 90 days: Analytics and performance cookies
  • 1 year: Preference and functional cookies
  • 2 years: Google Analytics cookies

5. Managing Your Cookie Preferences

5.1 Browser Settings

You can control cookies through your browser settings:

  • Chrome: Settings → Privacy and security → Cookies and other site data
  • Firefox: Options → Privacy & Security → Cookies and Site Data
  • Safari: Preferences → Privacy → Cookies and website data
  • Edge: Settings → Cookies and site permissions → Cookies and site data

5.2 Cookie Consent Tool

When you first visit our site, you'll see a cookie banner where you can:

  • Accept all cookies
  • Reject non-essential cookies
  • Customize your preferences by cookie category

You can change your preferences anytime by clicking the "Cookie Settings" link in our footer.

5.3 Opt-Out Links

  • Google Analytics Opt-Out: Browser Add-on
  • Network Advertising Initiative: Opt-Out Tool
  • Digital Advertising Alliance: Consumer Choice Page

6. Do Not Track (DNT)

Our website does not currently respond to "Do Not Track" signals from browsers because there is no universally accepted standard for how to respond to such signals.

However, you can still control tracking through:

  • Browser cookie settings
  • Privacy-focused browser extensions
  • Our cookie consent tool

7. Mobile Devices

7.1 Mobile SDKs & App Tracking

Our mobile applications may use similar technologies:

  • Device identifiers (IDFA on iOS, Advertising ID on Android)
  • Local storage and cache
  • Analytics SDKs (Firebase, Amplitude)

7.2 Managing Mobile Tracking

  • iOS: Settings → Privacy → Tracking → Limit Ad Tracking
  • Android: Settings → Google → Ads → Opt out of Ads Personalization

8. Impact of Disabling Cookies

If you disable cookies, some features may not work properly:

8.1 Disabling Essential Cookies

You may not be able to:

  • Log in to your account
  • Save preferences or settings
  • Use secure areas of the website
  • Complete purchases or transactions

8.2 Disabling Performance Cookies

  • We can't improve user experience based on analytics
  • Technical issues may take longer to identify and fix

8.3 Disabling Functional Cookies

  • Preferences won't be remembered between visits
  • Personalized content may not be available
  • Chat history won't be saved

8.4 Disabling Marketing Cookies

  • You'll still see ads, but they won't be personalized
  • We can't measure ad campaign effectiveness

9. Updates to This Policy

We may update this Cookie Policy from time to time to reflect:

  • Changes in our cookie practices
  • New technologies or services
  • Legal or regulatory requirements
  • User feedback and best practices

We will notify you of material changes by:

  • Updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this page
  • Displaying a prominent notice on our website
  • Sending an email to registered users (for significant changes)
